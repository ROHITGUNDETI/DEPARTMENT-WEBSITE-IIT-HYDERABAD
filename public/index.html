Department of Climate Change

The Department of Climate Change at the IIT Hyderabad attempts to explore climate change integrating academic knowledge with practical knowledge bringing scientists, engineers, practitioners, and students together. The key is an understanding of the strong association between the basic climate sciences, the technology & engineering solutions, and the policy. We, at IITH, plan to be a leading institute in the synergy among these three key areas. This clearly highlights the need for multi-disciplinary courses. We plan to achieve this with a unique curriculum taking the help of IITH fractal academics programme. The curriculum involves core courses, elective courses, seminar series by the experts of various disciplines, focus group discussions, field visits, and research thesis

Climate Change

The Department of Climate Change at IIT Hyderabad is dedicated to tackling the pressing global impacts of climate change, such as rising temperatures , and extreme weather events. Recognizing the urgent need for interdisciplinary solutions, the department brings together scientists, engineers, practitioners, and students. By integrating climate sciences, technology, engineering, social policy research,sustainable practices, and supported by IITH's innovative fractal academics program, the department offers a comprehensive curriculum. This includes core and elective courses, expert seminars, and research thesis. The department fosters collaborative environment, enhanced knowledge, technological innovations, sustainable practices, and business opportunities.
