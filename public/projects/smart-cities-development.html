    Title: Realizing a modal shift of urban transportation towards a low-carbon society through traffic analysis

    Description: With the rapid economic growth in emerging countries, particularly India, the negative impacts of increasing transportation such as environmental disruption and fatal accidents have become a growing social concern. The actual traffic conditions are difficult to grasp at present. The project aims to grasp the situation of urban traffic by analyzing big data collected with the latest sensing technologies. The objective is to realize a low-carbon, smart-mobility society by building a multi-modal system which utilizing appropriate transportation in city with more usage of public transportation with sufficient feeder.

    Project Status: Ongoing

    Cost: India Side: 458.92 Lakh (Revised at the 4th JCC, Oct 2019)

    Start Date: April 1 2017

    End Date: March 31 2022
