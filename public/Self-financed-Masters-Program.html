Self-financed Masters Program in Climate Change:

Department of Climate Change, IIT Hyderabad introduces a 2-year MTech program under self-financed mode as an opportunity for students who have not qualified GATE. The self-financed program is identical to our existing 2-year M.Tech (MHRD-funded) Program in all academic aspects.
